About

I'm a multidisciplinary systems engineer with hands-on experience delivering hardware and software research systems for NASA-funded projects and particle accelerator labs. My work spans the full product lifecycle — from concept and CAD modeling through prototyping, testing, and documentation.

Most recently, I led engineering and research for the NASA MSTAR project, engineered a Power-over-Fiber experimental platform at SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory, and designed College of the Desert's first CubeSat mission.
